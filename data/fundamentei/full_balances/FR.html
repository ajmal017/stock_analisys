Description
First Industrial Realty Trust is a real estate investment trust engaged in acquiring, owning, and managing industrial real estate throughout the U.S. First Industrial's real estate portfolio is primarily comprised of light industrial properties, which are split between industrial and office space, and bulk warehouse properties generally located in business parks near transportation hubs.
