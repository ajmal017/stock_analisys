Description
Founded in 1999, MercadoLibre operates online commerce marketplaces (representing around 48% of revenue in 2018) in more than a dozen Latin American countries, while also offering an online/offline payment-processing platform (MercadoPago), credit solutions for buyers/sellers, display and paid search advertising capabilities (MercadoClics), online store management services (MercadoShops), and third-party logistics solutions (MercadoEnvios).
